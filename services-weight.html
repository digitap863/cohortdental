Welcome to AYUR FYT Weight Management, where wellness meets wisdom in a holistic approach to achieving your health and weight goals. Our journey together begins with an exploration of Ayurveda, the ancient science of life. At AYUR FYT, we believe in the balance of mind, body, and spirit, and our experienced Ayurvedic practitioners are here to tailor a personalized plan to your unique constitution (Dosha).

Contact us today to schedule your consultation and take the first step towards a healthier, happier you. Your well-being is our priority, and we're here to support you every step of the way. Welcome to AYUR FYT - where wellness meets wisdom.

Frequently Asked Questions
Why choose AYUR FYT?

We offer a comprehensive approach that includes the wisdom of Ayurveda, the power of yoga, personalized diet plans, herbal support, and a focus on mind-body balance.

What strategies are in place for maintaining weight loss and preventing relapse?

Our program focuses on equipping you with the knowledge and skills to maintain your weight loss in the long term. We emphasize a sustainable approach to weight management.

Can you explain the approach to physical activity and exercise recommendations within the program?

We provide exercise recommendations tailored to your fitness level, preferences, and any medical considerations. The goal is to incorporate physical activity that complements your weight management goals

Let's dive into the key elements of our holistic approach:

  • Ayurvedic Wisdom : Our Ayurvedic practitioners assess your Dosha to create a customized plan that aligns with your specific needs. Ayurveda emphasizes balance, and we integrate this ancient wisdom into every aspect of your weight management journey.

  • Yoga for Weight Management : Our certified yoga instructors guide you through practices that boost metabolism, improve digestion, and reduce stress. Yoga is not just about physical postures; it's a holistic practice that enhances your overall well-being.

  • Personalized Diet Plans : Our nutritionists work closely with you to create a diet plan aligned with your Ayurvedic constitution and weight management goals. We believe in nourishing your body with whole, natural foods, recognizing that one size doesn't fit all in nutrition.

  • Herbal Support : Harnessing the power of herbs, we incorporate Ayurvedic remedies to support weight loss and overall health, complementing your holistic journey.
